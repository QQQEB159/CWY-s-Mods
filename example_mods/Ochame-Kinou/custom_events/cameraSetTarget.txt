cameraSetTarget(character:String, ?loop:Bool = false):Void

Changes the Follow Point to target a character.

☉ character - Can be "bf", "gf", or "dad"("opponent" alternatively).
☉ loop(Optional) - If true, The camera will be fixed and locked on the character.

Examples:
	☉ cameraSetTarget('opponent');
	- Sets camera follow point to Opponent.
